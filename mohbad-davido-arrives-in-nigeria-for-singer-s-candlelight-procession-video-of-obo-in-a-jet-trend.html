Mohbad: Davido Arrives in Nigeria for Singers Candlelight Procession, Video of OBO in a Jet Trend

September 13, 2024
blog
  • Davido has finally arrived in Nigeria hours before the candlelight procession and tribute concert for the late singer Mohbad
  • The DMW label boss made headlines after he revealed he would fly back to Nigeria for the event
  • Davido has now fulfilled his promise, as many of his fans and well-wishers have taken to social media to applaud him

Briefly News can confirm that Nigerian international act David Adeleke, aka Davido, has arrived in Nigeria for the candlelight procession and tribute concert set to take place in Lekki, Lagos, on Thursday, 21 September in honour of the late Mohbad.

It was earlier reported that Davido had taken to his Instagram story to reveal he would fly back to the country for the event.

Davido has been one of the popular celebrities in the country who have shown support for the late Mohbad, including reaching out to his family after his demise.

The DMW boss shared clips of him stepping out of a jet as he arrived in the country as his close associate and logistic manager, Isreal DMW, could be heard hailing him.
