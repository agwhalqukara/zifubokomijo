Andi Dorfman: I've Felt &quot;Love and Happiness&quot; Since Josh Murray Split

September 30, 2024
blog

She may not have a fiance, but she has plenty of friends and fans. Andi Dorfman thanked those who've supported her since splitting from Josh Murray in an Instagram post on Wednesday, Feb. 18.

"In a journey that changed my life, I have felt the strongest love and happiness but also the darkest sorrow and pain," the Bachelorette alum's message read. "But I am alive. I am standing."

"Not alone, but surrounded by every stranger's kind word. By every immeasurable ounce of love from family and friends," the 27-year-old continued. "I have searched for the words to express my gratitude but can't seem to find them. So I will just two two…THANK YOU."

The former Atlanta Assistant District Attorney and Murray, 30, called off their five-month engagement last month. Earlier this week, she opened up about the couple's decision in a sit-down interview with Bachelor host Chris Harrison on a special that aired this past Sunday. 

"[There was] nothing official before that, obviously to make the decision to end the engagement doesn't just happen overnight. We had struggled for a while — we definitely did," she tearfully said, noting that the breakup has been the biggest failure of her life. "We were both very aware of the fact that we just didn't better each other. I didn't feel supported and empowered, and I don't think I gave him the support and empowerment that he needed."

"I'll always love him, like I've never loved anybody like that in my life," she added of the former baseball player. "That was my first true love, without a doubt."
