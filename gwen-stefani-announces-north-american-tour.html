Gwen Stefani Announces North American Tour

August 26, 2024
blog

Gwen Stefani has announced her “This is What the Truth Feels Like” North American tour beginning this July in Boston, Massachusetts.

Gwen will be accompanied by opening guest EVE for every show throughout the entire tour. The North American tour will run from July until October stopping in numerous locations throughout the U.S. such as Tampa, Chicago, Dallas, and Vegas.

For those who are Citi® cardmembers, pre sale tickets will be available starting on Tuesday, April 19th. For the general public, ticket sales will begin on Friday, April 22nd.

The “This is What the Truth Feels Like” tour will also have limited amount of VIP tickets for purchase which include meet and greet with the artist, exclusive merchandise, and premium seating for the show.
