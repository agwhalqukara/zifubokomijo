Katie Price fans all say same thing as daughter Princess poses for fashion ad on Instagram

KATIE Price’s fans gushed how her eldest Princess was her “absolute double” as she posed for an Instagram snap.

Princess plumped for drawstring joggers and a matching sleeveless vest top, which she paired with pink fluffy sliders.

She turned side-profile for her shot, as she posed by a caramel-coloured wall.

She made sure her make up was perfect and added lashings of black mascara.
