Clarksville Veterinary Emergency & Specialty seeks an Emergency Veterinarian to join our team (generous relocation bonus offered).

We are interested in speaking with internship-trained, experienced, or general practice veterinarians with a passion for emergency medicine for Part Time or Full Time positions.

We opened in April 2020 and are rapidly growing closer to our goal of 24/7 ER receiving! On-site specialists include Surgery and Internal Medicine 4 days per week. Access to our specialists at the Nashville location is also available!

We also have a growing internship program and many of our interns stay with us as emergency doctors.

Our specialty equipment includes on site CT, MRI, digital radiography, ultrasound, echocardiography, laparoscopy, endoscopy, cystoscopy, and electrodiagnostics. We are also deeply committed to environmental sustainability and supporting our community through volunteer work.

In addition to a competitive salary, CVES offers a comprehensive benefits package and relocation bonus. We are looking for hard working and compassionate individuals, with broad abilities and a strong desire to contribute to our family and team environment. CVES focuses on continually working to improve the quality of care we provide our patients, the client experience, and the health of our team. We strive to create balance with work and home life, as well as promoting respect and clear communication between everyone in our organization.

Clarksville was just named the #1 best place to live by Money Magazine ! Clarksville boasts a smaller community feel with a short drive to Nashville, good schools, and is a great place to buy a home.

Please visit our website ! To learn more, please apply online or submit your resume/CV to Sarah Harvey at sarah.harvey@ethosvet.com.

About Clarksville Veterinary Emergency & Specialty

Ethos Veterinary Health (“Ethos”) consists of over 145 world-class specialty and emergency hospitals dedicated to advancing animal health and providing exceptional, compassionate care. With a network of over 2,000 specialty and emergency caregivers, Ethos brings together an integrated community of expertise and collaboration enabling continuous growth and learning. As one of the largest veterinary specialty networks, Ethos takes a scientific, evidence-based approach to lead the advancement of cutting-edge medicine to extend and improve the lives of pets. For more information, visit www.ethosvet.com.
