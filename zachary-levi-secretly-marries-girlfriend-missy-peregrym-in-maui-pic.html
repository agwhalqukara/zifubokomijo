Zachary Levi Secretly Marries Girlfriend Missy Peregrym In Maui: Pic

September 23, 2024
blog

It’s official! Zachary Levi is a married man!

The Chuck actor and his girlfriend Missy Peregrym revealed on their Twitter accounts on June 16 that they said “I do” in Hawaii.

Peregrym posted a picture of her and her beau in matching “Mr.” and “Mrs.” sweatshirts to break the news, along with the caption: “That coffee date was UNREAL @zacharylevi #marriedinmaui.” Levi retweeted the pic, adding the caption: “These dreams and goals are really workin out.”

The couple’s coffee joke began days before on June 9, when the Rookie Blue star tweeted: “Hey @ZacharyLevi, great job at the Tony’s. You’re a real babe alert. How about we grab a coffee and talk about your dreams and goals?”

“Hey, thank you,” Zachary flirted back, “Name the place and time. I’ll bring my own mug. Not my typical move, but I’m feelin like takin a chance.”

Good pal and Avatar actor Joel David Moore was also in on the discreet nuptials. Moore took to his own Twitter to congratulate the couple on June 16. “Congrats to @ZacharyLevi and @mperegrym on their stunning wedding and beautiful future,” he wrote, “Couldn’t be happier for my brother & my new sis pal.”
